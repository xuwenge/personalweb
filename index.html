I am a lecturer in HCI at Birmingham City University. My research interests are in HCI, focusing on XR (VR/AR/MR), UX, 3DUI, Serious Games.

I received my PhD at the University of Liverpool, where I was supervised by Prof Hai-Ning Liang. My PhD studies were supported by a Postgraduate Research Scholarship (XJTLU: RDF 15-02-12).

I am actively looking to recruit postgraduate students who want to do HCI/XR/Games projects with me. Feel free to contact me.
