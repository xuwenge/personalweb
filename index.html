Wenge Xu | 徐温格

I'm a lecturer in HCI at the Birmingham City University (see my BCU page). I obtained my PhD in 2021 from University of Liverpool, where I was supervised by Prof Hai-Ning Liang. My PhD studies were supported by a Postgraduate Research Scholarship (XJTLU: RDF 15-02-12).

My research area is HCI where my current research interests are XR (VR/AR/MR), UX, 3DUI, Serious Games (see my Google Scholar profile). I am particularly interested in researching immersive offices and immersive exercises and rehabilitation. Meanwhile, if you are students who want to build up your CV via doing projects with me, feel free to contact me, I am also actively looking to recruit postgraduate students.
