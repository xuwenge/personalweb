Wenge Xu | 徐温格

I'm a lecturer in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at the Birmingham City University (see my BCU page). I obtained my PhD in 2021 from University of Liverpool, where I was supervised by Prof Hai-Ning Liang.


My research mainly focuses on HCI, especially, I research on Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), Games (see my Google Scholar profile). Open for collaboration.
