Wenge Xu

I am a lecturer in HCI and an active researcher at the DMT Lab. My research interests are HCI, XR (VR, AR, MR), User Experience, 3D User Interface, Serious Games.

I earned my PhD in 2021 at the University of Liverpool, where I was supervised by Prof Hai-Ning Liang. My PhD studies were supported by a Postgraduate Research Scholarship (XJTLU: RDF 15-02-12).

I have published roughly 20 International journals and conferences full papers. Many of these publications are published in top-tier HCI/XR conferences like ACM CHI, IEEE VR, IEEE ISMAR; top-ranked journals like IEEE TVCG, JMIR serious games, Games for Health. In addition, I am a active reviewer for leading conferences (e.g., ACM CHI, IEEE VR, IEEE ISMAR) and journals (IJHCS, JMIR serious games, IEEE Transactions on Games).
