Wenge Xu | 徐温格

I'm a lecturer in HCI at the Birmingham City University (see my BCU page). I obtained my PhD in 2021 from University of Liverpool, where I was supervised by Prof Hai-Ning Liang. My PhD studies were supported by a Postgraduate Research Scholarship (XJTLU: RDF 15-02-12).


My research area is HCI where my current research interests are XR (VR/AR/MR), UX, 3DUI, Serious Games (see my Google Scholar profile).
