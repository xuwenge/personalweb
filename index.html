Wenge Xu

I am a lecturer in HCI at Birmingham City University and an active researcher at the DMT Lab. My research interests are in HCI focusing on XR (VR, AR, MR), User Experience, 3D User Interface, Serious Games.

I earned my PhD in 2021 at the University of Liverpool, where I was supervised by Prof Hai-Ning Liang. My PhD studies were supported by a Postgraduate Research Scholarship (XJTLU: RDF 15-02-12).

I have published roughly 20 International journals and conferences full papers at leading venus like IEEE TVCG, JMIR serious games, Games for Health, ACM CHI, IEEE VR, IEEE ISMAR. In addition, I am a active reviewer for leading conferences (e.g., ACM CHI, IEEE VR, IEEE ISMAR) and journals (IJHCS, JMIR serious games, IEEE Transactions on Games).
